Pet Care Knowledge

1. What should you consider before adopting a pet?

Before adopting a pet, you should consider your time, financial ability, living environment, and long-term responsibility.

2. How to help a new pet adapt to your home?

A newly adopted pet may feel nervous at first. Give them a quiet, safe space and allow them to adjust gradually.

3. Regular health care

Pets need regular health checks, vaccinations, parasite prevention, proper grooming, and daily care.
